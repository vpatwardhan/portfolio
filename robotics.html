Robotics

Breadboard

This was my first opportunity to create a complete circuit. Additionally, using an Arduino we were able to make a light flash, as well as to make two lights alternate. We also experimented with making lights turn on and off depending on when a switch was pressed.

Rupert Jennifer

This robot was coded using modified C++ in Arduino. When the robot hit an object, the whisker on that side would contact a sensor and interrupt the circuit, and in response the robot would move backward and then turn in the opposite direction to avoid the obstacle. This allowed Rupert Jennifer to navigate through a maze in 21 seconds.
